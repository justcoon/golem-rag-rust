Introduction to Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. It focuses on developing computer programs that can access data and use it to learn for themselves.

Key Concepts:
- Supervised Learning: Learning from labeled training data
- Unsupervised Learning: Finding patterns in unlabeled data
- Reinforcement Learning: Learning through interaction with environment
- Deep Learning: Neural networks with multiple layers

Applications of machine learning include:
1. Image recognition and computer vision
2. Natural language processing
3. Speech recognition
4. Recommendation systems
5. Autonomous vehicles
6. Medical diagnosis
7. Fraud detection

The future of machine learning looks promising with advancements in quantum computing, edge AI, and more sophisticated algorithms being developed continuously.
